Introducing the Budget Hub

Once you’re set up, the SpendAble Budget Hub becomes your go-to destination to:

  • See how support workers are linked to participants
  • View any transaction, including receipts
  • Create and edit budgets

Your customers and support workers receive their own individual view of the Budget Hub – all data is stored securely and displayed privately.

The Budget Hub will replace the need for any cashbooks and ledgers, storing all receipts or evidence of payment against each transaction.
