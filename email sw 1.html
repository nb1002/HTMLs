How does it work?

SpendAble delivers a robust solution for compliant, secure money handling in four steps:

  1. Participants and support workers are linked to a service provider’s account
  2. Trusted supports are linked to their participant’s wallet
  3. Both supports and participants transact freely and securely
  4. You and participants maintain real time oversight and transparency

The end result? You spend less time managing other people’s money and more time doing what you do best.
